Raspberry Pi RC Car

The goal of this project was to explore robotics and challenge myself to create an rc car that is controlled with a Wii controller. The rc car consisted of a Raspberry Pi, four dc motors, dual H bridge, battery pack, and a chassis. Using python, I was able to connect the Wii controller to the Pi via bluetooth.
